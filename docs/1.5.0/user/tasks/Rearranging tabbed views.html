Rearranging tabbed views¶

In addition to dragging and dropping views on the Workbench, the order of views can also be rearranged within a tabbed notebook.

  1. Click on the Layers title bar and drag it on top of the Projects view. The Layers will now be stacked on top of the Projects.

  2. Click on the Projects tab and drag it to the right of the Layers tab.

  3. Once the cursor is to the right of the Layers tab and the cursor is a stack cursor release the mouse button.

  4. Observe that the Projects tab is now to the right of the Locals tab.

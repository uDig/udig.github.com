Try It Out¶

  • Select the Overview tab

  • Click the synchronize link in the Testing section

  • Click the Launch an Eclipse application link, check that your splash screen with progress bar and progress message works

  • Drag a few files on to the screen and try out the application.

  • The application defaults to our Custom perspective, to switch perspectives use Window ‣ Open Perspective ‣ Other

  • Open up Help ‣ About and have a look at the branding info.

  • Use ALT + TAB to switch between windows, look at the window icon.

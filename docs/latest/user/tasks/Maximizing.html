Maximizing¶

Sometimes it is useful to be able to maximize a view or editor. Maximizing both views and editors is easy.

  1. To maximize a view, either double click on its tab or choose Maximize from the tab’s popup menu.
  2. To maximize an editor, double click on the editor tab or choose Maximize from the tab’s popup menu.
  3. Restoring a view to its original size is done in a similar manner (double click or choose Restore from the menu).

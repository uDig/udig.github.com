Show or hide background layers¶

Hiding background layers¶

  1. Click on the Hide background layers button on the view’s toolbar
  2. The view will hide all the background layers from the list

Showing background layers¶

  1. Click on the Show background layers button on the view’s toolbar
  2. The view will show all the background layers from the list
